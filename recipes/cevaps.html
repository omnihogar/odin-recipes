Chevapchichi

A traditional and well beloved balkan dish, can be found over most of south-eastern Europe, but most famously in Bosnia and Herzegovina, where it is considered a national dish.

It contains the following ingridients:

  • Beef (young beef, preferably veal) 1.5 kg
  • Lamb 0.5 kg
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • Sodium Bicarbonate 2tsp

Instructions:

  1. Cut meat to 2 x 2 cm cubes and salt to 1.8-2% ratio
  2. Boil garlic in 100 ml of water, let cool and pour over meat
  3. Let meat cool over night in the fridge
  4. Grind meat 2 times to medium size with a fi 6 mm grate, mix well
  5. Leave ceovered for 4-5 hours, can be kept for up to 2 days in the fridge after this
  6. Add bicarbonate of soda and mix well
  7. Shape the chevapi to 5 cm length, 1 cm width, press them well to make them come together
  8. Grill over hot flame, preferably wood fueled, keep the grate oiled with lamb lard (preerably), lard or oil
  9. Cut buns in 2, lightly dip in beef soup, steam on the grill over the chevapi
  10. Serve when chevapi are still a little pink in the middle
